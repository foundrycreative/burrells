Supplies

Keeping You Well Stocked.

We offer a range of industrial supplies, including lockers and consumables, first aid and hygiene supplies through to floor care and washroom and hand care supplies.

Workplace products Supplied by Apparelmaster

Standards assessed and certified by AsureQuality

What do you need?

First Aid
Supplies

We stock first aid kits and supplies for the workplace, individual workstations and vehicles. Our medical supplies are hospital grade and meet workplace health and safety standards.

Lockers

Our lockers are suitable for the range of workwear we supply. They are straightforward to install, fully lockable and come in a range of sizes and colours.

Floorcare
and Mats

We offer standard floor mats for entranceways and specialty mats for food preparation areas, workshops, wet areas and ramps. All in a range of colours, sizes, styles and materials such as rubber and non-slip.

Washroom
Supplies

We stock a range of washroom supplies from consumables such as soaps and hand sanitisers to dispensers, sanitary disposal units, towel systems and toilet paper dispensers.

Industrial
Towels

In this range are commercial-grade, durable towels suited to most workplaces. In the range are tea towels, cabinet roller towels and hand towels, as well as bath towels.
