Our products

We source all our workwear, supplies and linen from two industry leaders – Apparelmaster and Linenmaster. We trust their products, standards of care and we’re extremely proud to be associated with their brands. We think you’ll feel the same.

Your products

Sometimes our clients have workwear, supplies or linen on hand and simply want us to manage the cleaning, repair and storage of their workplace essentials. That’s fine with us – we’re happy to work in whatever way suits you. Rely on us to tailor our service to your business, while maintaining a high level of quality and care.

Why choose Burrell's?

Service

We’re a friendly, can-do team who deliver on time, every time. We’re committed to getting you the high-quality garment or product you need, in great condition and ready to use, at an excellent price.
