Burrell's Self-service Laundromats.

Burrell’s laundromats provide everyday convenience for people looking for fast, affordable, self-service laundry solutions. We have a range of high performance washers and energy efficient dryers designed to accommodate all of your domestic laundry needs. Our range of machines can handle small or large items ranging from clothes to bedding and uniforms.

Easy to Use

Our self-service machines are easy to use with coin operation (Eftpos option coming soon). Simply follow the in-store instructions and wash or dry your clothes with confidence.

Fast Turnaround

Our high performance, high capacity machines provide efficient wash and dry cycles meaning you spend less time waiting. 39 minute wash time, 20 minute dry time.

Convenient

We are open 5am till 11pm, 7 days. This means you can take care of your laundry before, during or after work at your convenience. The laundry closes automatically at 11pm. If you happen to leave clothes in the washers or dryers you can pick them up the next day.

Environmentally Friendly

Our front loader eco washing machines provide the best care for your clothes with the lowest possible water use. Our dryers are efficient with low energy consumption, giving you peace of mind that your laundering has less of an impact on the environment.
